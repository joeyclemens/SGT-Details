Welcome to SGT Details Car Cleaning & Detailing Services

SGT Details: Car Cleaning & Detailing Services is a fully equipped Auto Finesse accredited, trained & insured mobile detailer. With the necessary equipment, knowledge and experience to offer a high-quality service for your vehicle. SGT details values the customer and their needs, working with them closely and helping them achieve the desired look and feel for their vehicle. Before any type of service please ensure your vehicle is emptied of any personal belongings.

Full Day Paint Enhancement

£350-£550 depending on vehicle size

Full Day Paint Enhancement
(Duration up to 14hrs)

Our full paint enhancement is a whole day dedicated to machine polishing the paint work, removing those unsightly swirl marks and paint defects. We aim to remove 75-90% of imperfections, though deeper defects may remain.

• Alloys, arches and tyres thoroughly cleaned and dressed
• Engine bay degreased and dressed
• A Full exterior safe wash consisting of citrus snow foam prior to any contact with the vehicle
• Two bucket wash method
• Door shuts, boot sills and intricate areas will be cleaned
• The vehicle will then be decontaminated and clay bard to remove any iron particles and tar residue.
• A paint depth reading will be carried out across the whole car to get a clear indication and understanding of the clearcoat we are dealing with.
• A full single stage machine polish will be carried out removing up to 90% of defects in the paintwork (deeper defects in some cases will remain)

Protection

• The vehicles paint work will then be protected with a ceramic infused wax as standard which lasts from 6-8 months
